# Literacy Statistics - Kurukshetra District

## Overall Literacy Rate
**76.31%** (Census 2011)

## Gender-wise Literacy

- **Male Literacy:** 82.5%
- **Female Literacy:** 69.8%
- **Gender Gap:** 12.7 percentage points

## Location-wise Literacy

- **Urban Literacy:** 81.3%
- **Rural Literacy:** 73.2%
- **Urban-Rural Gap:** 8.1 percentage points

## Age Group Literacy

- **Youth (15-24 years):** 88.4%
- **Adults (25-59 years):** 74.2%
- **Elderly (60+ years):** 42.8%

## Educational Attainment

- **Primary (Class 1-5):** 12.8%
- **Middle (Class 6-8):** 18.5%
- **Secondary (Class 9-10):** 24.3%
- **Higher Secondary:** 16.2%
- **Graduate and above:** 15.7%

## Educational Infrastructure

- **Total Schools:** 847
- **Primary Schools:** 612
- **Secondary Schools:** 235
- **Government Schools:** 623
- **Private Schools:** 224

## Higher Education

- Kurukshetra University (established 1956)
- Multiple colleges and institutes
- Research centers

## Source
Census 2011, Department of Education Haryana
