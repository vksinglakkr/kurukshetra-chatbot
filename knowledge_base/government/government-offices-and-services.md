3. **Shahabad SDM**
   - Location: Shahabad town
   - Services: Sub-division administration

1. **Thanesar Tehsil**
   - Location: Thanesar
   - Services: Land records, revenue certificates, mutations, Fard

2. **Pehowa Tehsil**
   - Location: Pehowa
   - Services: Same as above for Pehowa area

3. **Shahabad Tehsil**
   - Location: Shahabad
   - Services: Same as above for Shahabad area

#### Services Provided

- Property registration appointments
