# Infrastructure & Development - Kurukshetra

## Road Connectivity

### National Highways

#### 1. NH-44 (Delhi-Amritsar)
- Passes through Kurukshetra district
- Major north-south corridor
- 6-lane in some sections
- **Length in district:** ~85 km

#### 2. NH-152 (Narwana-Ambala)
- Connects to neighboring districts
- 4-lane highway
- **Length in district:** ~40 km

### State Highways
- Multiple state highways
- Connect major towns and villages
- **Total length:** ~120 km
- Well-maintained by PWD

### District Roads
- **Total length:** ~450 km
- Connect blocks and tehsils
- Paved roads

### Village Roads
- **Total length:** 800+ km
- PMGSY (Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana) roads
- Connectivity to all villages

### Road Density
High compared to state average

### Major Road Projects
- Widening of NH-44 sections
- Bypass roads around major towns
- Flyovers at key junctions
- Road safety improvements

## Railway Connectivity

### Major Railway Stations

#### 1. Kurukshetra Junction (KKDE)
- Main station
- **Code:** KKDE
- All major trains stop
- **Facilities:** Waiting rooms, retiring rooms, platform amenities

#### 2. Thanesar City (TRR)
- Near city center
- Suburban services
- Some express trains

#### 3. Shahabad (Haryana) (SHD)
- Important junction
- Connects to Ambala-Saharanpur line

#### 4. Pehowa (PEH)
- Small station
- Local passenger services

### Railway Lines
- Delhi-Ambala main line (electrified)
- Part of Northern Railway zone
- Double track
- High-speed trains operate

### Connectivity

#### Direct Trains To:
- **Delhi:** 2-3 hours
- **Chandigarh:** 1.5-2 hours
- **Amritsar:** 3-4 hours
- Lucknow
- Jammu
- Haridwar
- Multiple daily services

### Train Categories
- Shatabdi Express
- Intercity Express
- Passenger trains
- Mail/Express trains

### Facilities
- Computerized reservation
- Unreserved ticketing
- Food stalls
- Parking
